# Overview
Join builders from across the world to create the next wave of industry-defining applications onchain

What is Base Batches?
Base Batches is a program designed to help builders kickstart their business.

This is your chance to get noticed, get funded, and get the support you need to bring your idea to market.

Base Batches this year will feature two dedicated tracks:

Startup Track: For early-stage teams. Generally, we’re looking for teams that are anywhere from pre-product, pre-launch, or pre-seed and have raised less than ~$250K. The top 15 teams will receive a $10K grant, acceptance to an 8-week virtual program, access to Base builder hubs, and a spot to present at Demo Day in San Francisco this May. A minimum of 3 teams will receive a $50K investment from the Base Ecosystem Fund (subject to the fund’s diligence review).
Student Track: Open to students from around the world. Teams can only include undergraduate students, however a single team can comprise students from multiple universities. The top 5 teams will be flown to San Francisco and present during Demo Day.
This is the page for the Student Track.

About the Student Track
The Base team is excited to add a dedicated student track to Base Batches. Undergraduate students from around the world may form teams and submit their application by April 27th to be eligible. The Base team will select the 5 top overall teams and fly up to 2 team members from each team out to San Francisco to join us for Demo Day.

This is not a hackathon. It doesn’t matter if you had previously written code for the project you ultimately submit.

Prizes
The top 5 teams will fly to San Francisco to join the Startup track finalists in presenting during Demo Day.

Each winning team will have all expenses covered for two team members.

Demo Day
All teams will then join us for Demo Day live from San Francisco. Teams will then present to a live audience with investors, VCs and accelerators who we invite to attend. A minimum of 3 teams will receive a $50K investment from the Base Ecosystem Fund (subject to the fund’s diligence review). All teams will be considered for investment by Coinbase Ventures.

What is Base Batches?
Base Batches is a program designed to help founders build, launch, and grow startups on Base.


Who should join Base Batches?
Whether you’re a solo founder with an idea, a small team with a prototype, or a web2 developer looking to break into web3, or a startup already gaining traction, you’re encouraged to join Base Batches.
Pre-product teams should have a strong founder-market fit if applying.


Do I need to be working on Base already?
Not necessarily. We welcome teams from across the ecosystem, and those who are building onchain for the first time, but you should be committed to launching and scaling on Base as part of the program.


What types of projects will get accepted?
We will accept a wide range of onchain projects, including:

Consumer apps
Mini apps
Defi apps and protocols
AI agents
However, your project should be largely onchain either driving transactions via usage or asset issuance.


Where does the program take place?
The program is primarily remote and global; however, we may bring together admitted teams for IRL gatherings and to help cohort members within a region meet fellow members. Teams that are invited to Demo Day will need to attend live in San Francisco.


How does Demo Day work?
Demo Day is your chance to showcase what you’ve built to a curated audience of investors, partners, and other ecosystem leaders. You’ll have the opportunity to pitch, share traction, and connect with the audience members who may be interested in supporting your business.


Do I need to have an incorporated company?
Not necessarily - it isn’t a hard requirement.


Will Base take equity or tokens?
Admitted teams into the startup track will receive grants which are equity-free. Teams that receive Base Ecosystem funding will be offered $50k investment with our standard deal terms (subject to the fund’s diligence process).


What happens after the program?
You’ll remain part of the Base founder community, with access to ongoing support, investor introductions, and opportunities to collaborate with new cohorts.
